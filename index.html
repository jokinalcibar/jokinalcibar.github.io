I'm a PhD Candidate at Mondragon University specializing in Artificial Intelligence, with a focus on developing Bayesian physics-guided neural networks for battery health assessment. My research combines probabilistic deep learning with physics-based models to improve diagnostic and prognostic accuracy in energy systems. With a background in industrial automation and power electronics, I bring practical engineering experience to my academic research.

I'm passionate about creating robust AI solutions that bridge the gap between data-driven approaches and physical system understanding, particularly for critical applications like battery health management in drones and industrial systems.
